Our Vision

Our mission is to revolutionize the way we approach driver
safety by prioritizing the physical and cognitive well-being
of those behind the wheel. We believe that by continuously
monitoring a driver's state of health, we can proactively
identify and address potential risks, ultimately preventing accidents and saving lives.

Our Team

Our team has developed a state-of-the-art Driver
Monitor Medical System that utilizes advanced
sensors, machine learning algorithms, and real-time
data analysis. This comprehensive system tracks a wide
range of physiological and behavioral indicators, such as
eye movements,blink rate, heart rate variability,
and steering patterns, to detect signs of impairment, fatigue, or distraction.
